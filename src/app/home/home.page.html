About INkosinathi School of Discovery

Our mission is to provide quality education and support to students through specialized programs designed to develop leadership skills and personal growth.

 Behavioural Change Support

Our behavioral change support program helps students develop positive habits and overcome challenges.

 School Prefect Support

We provide specialized training and mentorship for student leaders to develop their leadership abilities.

 Boys to Men Support

Our mentorship program focuses on guiding young men through positive role modeling and life skills development.

 Vezikhono Support Initiative

The Vezikhono initiative focuses on skills development and enhancing students' natural talents.

 Community Participation

We encourage students to engage with and contribute to their communities through various projects and initiatives.

 Lisekhona Ithemba Initiative

The Lisekhona Ithemba Initiative provides hope and support to students facing various life challenges.
